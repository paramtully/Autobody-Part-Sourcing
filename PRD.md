# Product Name (Working)
Partsync (placeholder)
---
# Problem:
Collision repair shops lose time and money sourcing parts due to fragmented supplier systems, inconsistent availability data, and unreliable ETAs.
---
# Users
## Primary:
- Independent collision repair shops
## Secondary:
- Parts distributors
- Salvage yards
- Insurance estimators
-
---
# Core Value Proposition
Enter VIN + damaged component → instantly get best available replacement option with price, ETA, and supplier reliability.
---
# Core Jobs To Be Done
## Shop:
- Find available replacement part fast
- Reduce repair delays
- Reduce phone calls / manual sourcing
-
---
# Success Metrics (First 6 Months)
## Product:
- Median search latency < 3s
- Vendor data freshness < 1 hour
- ≥ 3 vendor integrations live
## Business:
- 10 active weekly shops
- 80% weekly retention
- First paid customer
